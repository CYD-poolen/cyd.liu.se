System administrators are available for support during semesters every week from **monday to thursday from 17:15 to 18:00**.

During study breaks and examination periods there's no support available - but you can still reach sysadmin at admin@cyd.liu.se. During these periods we can't garantuee a quick response.

If you have question regarding CYD-poolen - please contact us through mail (admin@cyd.liu.se) or through our Facebook page ([CYD-poolen](https://www.facebook.com/CYDpoolen)).

### Mail
